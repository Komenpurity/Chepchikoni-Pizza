About Us

The first Chepchikoni Pizza outlet opened its doors in 1999 in Nairobi and rose quickly in popularity. Serving hot oven-fresh pizza with its signature all-natural tomato sauce and using only the freshest ingredients, Chepchikoni Pizza's has established itself at the top of the industry and is easily one of the first choices in quick service pizza in Africa.
